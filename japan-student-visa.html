Japan Student Visa Requirements

Comprehensive guide for Nepali students pursuing higher education in Japan

The **Japan Student Visa** is your gateway to world-class education. At Apex Consultancy, we specialize in high-precision documentation to ensure your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) and Visa approval remains among the highest in Nepal.

Understanding the Student Visa

International students must obtain a Student Visa (Ryugaku 留学). This visa allows you to stay in Japan for the duration of your study (typically 6 months to 2 years) and permits part-time work up to 28 hours per week.

The 4-Step Success Process

Step 1: College/School Application

Apply to your chosen Japanese Language School or University. Once accepted, the school applies for your COE in Japan.

Step 2: Obtain COE (Certificate of Eligibility)

  • Issued by the Japanese Immigration Bureau.
  • Takes 2–3 months to process.
  • This is the most critical document for your visa.

Step 3: Embassy Interview & Submission

Submit your COE and original documents to the Japanese Embassy in Panipokhari, Kathmandu. You may be required to attend an interview or a basic Japanese language test (NAT/JLPT).

Step 4: Visa Issuance

Standard processing at the Embassy takes about 5-7 working days after successful submission.

Document Checklist for Nepali Students

1. Educational Credentials

  • SEE/SLC & +2 Transcripts and Character Certificates.
  • Bachelor's Degree documents (if applying for PG/Masters).
  • Japanese Language Certificate (JLPT, NAT-TEST, or 150+ hours certificate).
  • Recommendation letter from the last institution.

2. Financial Documentation

  • Bank Balance Certificate (Recommended: 18–25 Lakh NPR).
  • 3–6 months Bank Statement showing consistent source of funds.
  • Relationship Certificate with Sponsor (Nata Pramanit).
  • Annual Income Certificate & Property Valuation.
  • Tax Clearance Certificates.

3. Personal Identification

  • Valid Passport (Machine Readable).
  • Citizenship Certificate (Translated).
  • Birth Certificate & Police Clearance Report.
  • Passport-sized photos (45mm x 45mm, white background).
Important: All Nepali documents must be translated into English or Japanese and must be authentic. Immigration is extremely strict regarding document integrity.

Embassy Interview Preparation

  • Be clear about your "Why Japan?" reasoning.
  • Know your school’s location and the course details perfectly.
  • Understand your sponsor's professional background.
  • Maintain basic conversational Japanese skills.
Expert Support: Apex Consultancy provides free mock interviews and document vetting to ensure your application is "Immigration Ready."

Visa Processing Timeline

  • Selection: 1 month
  • COE Wait: 2-3 months
  • Embassy: 1 week
  • Total: ~4-6 Months

VFS/Embassy Fee

The visa fee for Nepali students is approximately NPR 3,000 – 3,500 (subject to change by the Embassy of Japan in Nepal).

Japanese Language Course

JLPT & NAT-TEST (N5, N4, N3) Preparation - Nepal's Premier Japanese Language Training Center

Achieving a Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT) or NAT-TEST qualification is the primary requirement for Nepali students to secure a COE and Visa for Japan. At Apex Consultancy, we offer structured modules from N5 to N3 to ensure your success.

The Importance of Japanese Proficiency

For Nepali students and workers, language proficiency is not just a skill—it is a legal requirement for the visa process. High scores lead to:

  • High COE (Certificate of Eligibility) approval rates
  • Direct admission into prestigious Japanese Universities and Vocational Schools
  • Eligibility for various merit-based scholarships in Japan
  • Higher-paying part-time jobs (Baito) immediately upon arrival

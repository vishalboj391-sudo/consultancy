Cost of Studying in Japan

Complete cost calculation for studying in Japan from Bangladesh - City-wise analysis and budget planning

Many people have questions about Cost of Studying in Japan from Bangladesh. Although the first year costs 8-12 lakh taka, this expense can be managed to a large extent by doing part-time jobs. Detailed cost analysis is provided on this page.

Total Cost Breakdown (First Year)

Category Tokyo Osaka Other Cities
Tuition Fee (Language School) 5-6 lakh 4-5 lakh 3.5-4.5 lakh
Accommodation (Dormitory/Shared House) 3-4 lakh 2-3 lakh 1.5-2.5 lakh
Food 2-3 lakh 2-2.5 lakh 1.5-2 lakh
Transport 50-80 thousand 40-60 thousand 30-50 thousand
Other (Books, Utilities, Medical) 1-1.5 lakh 80 thousand-1 lakh 60-80 thousand
Total 12-15 lakh 9-12 lakh 7-10 lakh

City-wise Cost Analysis

Tokyo (Tokyo)

12-15 lakh taka/year

Being the capital, it has the highest cost. However, job opportunities are also the highest. Part-time job can earn 1.5-2 lakh yen per month.

Osaka (Osaka)

9-12 lakh taka/year

20-30% less expensive than Tokyo. Being a business city, jobs are easy to find. Living cost is reasonable.

Fukuoka (Fukuoka)

7-9 lakh taka/year

Rapidly growing international student friendly city. More government support. Low living cost.

Bank Statement Requirements

For visa application, you need to prove that you or your sponsor can bear this cost.

Required Funds:

  • Minimum: 15 lakh taka (in bank statement)
  • Ideal: 18-20 lakh taka
  • For sponsor: Sponsor's bank statement + income proof

Important: Don't deposit money suddenly, save gradually at least 3-6 months in advance.

Ways to Reduce Cost

  • Scholarship: Various government and private scholarships available (30-50 thousand yen per month)
  • Part-time Job: Working 28 hours per week can earn 1-1.5 lakh yen per month
  • Shared House: Cost becomes half if you take a roommate instead of living alone
  • Student Discount: You will get discounts on trains, restaurants, shopping
  • Food: Save 50% by cooking yourself instead of eating outside

Cost from Second Year

Setup cost is high in the first year. From the second year:

  • Tuition fee may decrease (if you get scholarship for good results)
  • Income from part-time job increases (if Japanese language improves)
  • Total cost can be reduced to 6-8 lakh taka
